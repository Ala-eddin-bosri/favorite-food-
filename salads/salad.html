caesar salad

Ingredients
iceberg lettuce
chicken breast
2 slices of bread
olive oil
mayonnaise 6 tbsp
lemon juice 1 tbsp
Dijon mustard 1 tbsp
salt
pepper
Garlic 1 clove
grated parmesan 1 tbsp
milk1 tbsp
PREPARATION
1/One hour before the meal, mix all the ingredients for the sauce. Film and leave to rest for an hour in the refrigerator.
2/ Cut the slices of bread into cubes. Fry the croutons in a drizzle of olive oil for 2 minutes and leave to cool.
3/Cut the chicken into small pieces and cook in a non-stick pan without fat. Brown and let cool.
4/Tear the lettuce and small pieces.
To finish Arrange in order: salad, chicken, croutons and Caesar sauce.

Carrots From The Moroccan salad

Ingredients for 4 persons
a bunch of broccoli (about 500 g)
800g potatoes
200 g of bacon
1 shallot
salt pepper
mustard
oil
vinegar
REPARATION
1/ Peel and cut the potatoes into cubes. Steam them.
2/Meanwhile: in a salad bowl, prepare a generous mustard vinaigrette. Peel and mince the shallot add to the vinaigrette.
3/Fry the bacon in a frying pan without fat. Meanwhile, cut the broccoli into small bouquets, add to the salad bowl. Mix immediately.
4/Add the browned bacon without the fat sweated during cooking.
To finish Cool the potatoes in cold water and add them to the salad bowl and mix.

Carrots From The Moroccan salad

Ingredients
carrot 4
lemon juice 1/2 cup
garlic 3 cloves
cummin 1 tsp
fresh coriander
olive oil
REPARATION
1/put the carrots into slices about 4mm thick.
2In a large saucepan, cook the carrots with a little water for 15 minutes.
3/Drain the carrots and then brown them in a pan with a little hot olive oil, add the cumin and lemon juice. To mix together. Turn off the heat and stir in the fresh coriander and garlic cloves.
To finish Leave to cool slowly in the pan so that the flavors mix well, stirring occasionally.
