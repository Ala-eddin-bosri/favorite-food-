Chocolate-banana tart

Ingredients for 6 people
1 shortcrust pastry
200g dark pastry chocolate
10 cl of liquid cream
4 bananas, peeled and cut into even slices
50g brown sugar
3 eggs
10 cl of liquid cream
For decoration: toasted slivered almonds or chocolate chips or icing sugar
PREPARATION
1/Bake the shortcrust pastry for 15 to 20 minutes. When it is golden, take it out and let it cool.
2/Mash the bananas and spread the puree over the bottom of the pie.
3/Melt the chocolate bar with the butter and add a little liquid cream to obtain a smooth mixture.
4/Pour the chocolate over the bananas and leave in the fridge for about 2 hours.
To finish Enjoy well chilled, accompanied by a scoop of chocolate, vanilla or banana ice cream.

Raspberry and grapefruit express dessert

Ingredients for 2 people
2 or 3 spoon cookies
2 teaspoons of amaretto (or rum)
1 grapefruit
30 raspberries (fresh or frozen)
1 Greek yogurt
1 handful of shelled pistachios, unsalted
a little sugar (optional)
PREPARATION
1/Cut the biscuits into pieces and garnish the bottom of the glasses.
2/Pour a teaspoon of amaretto into each glass, over the biscuits.
3/ Cut the grapefruit in half and cut it into small pieces.
4/Mix the yoghurt with a dozen raspberries. Add a little sugar if necessary (optional because cookies and fruit already add a sweet touch).
5/ Divide the grapefruit pieces into the glasses. Then add the yogurt and arrange the remaining raspberries on top.
To finish Decorate with crushed pistachios.
