4 cheese pizza

Ingredients for 4 persons
1 pizza dough
200 g special pizza tomato coulis
65g cheddar cheese
40g Comté cheese
50 g blue cheese (or gorgonzola)
1 ball of mozzarella
basil
PREPARATION
1/Roll out the pizza dough in a pizza pan.
2/Pour the tomato coulis and spread it over the dough using the back of a tablespoon, leaving about 1 cm without topping all around the pizza.
3/Cut the four cheeses into cubes and spread them over the pizza.
4/Sprinkle with basil.
To finish Bake for about 20 minutes at 170°C (thermostat 5/6).

Neapolitan pizza

Ingredients for 2 people
A roll of ready-made pizza dough (about 250 g)
A few anchovy fillets (5 to 10 according to taste)
A tablespoon of capers
100g grated cheese
A few black olives
For the tomato sauce:
1 can of tomato flesh, around 400 g
1 onion
1 clove of garlic
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 tablespoon of oregano
A few basil leaves
2 to 3 sugar cubes
Cayenne pepper
salt, ground pepper
PREPARATION
1/In a pan, heat the olive oil and throw in the detailed onion. Cook it over low heat until it becomes translucent. Add the detailed garlic at the end just long enough for it to brown very lightly without burning. Add the tomato flesh and bring to medium to high heat. Simmer for 20 minutes, adding oregano, basil, salt, pepper, cayenne pepper and sugar to taste. Lower the heat while stirring to reduce the sauce. Book.
2/Preheat the oven to 210°C.
3/Spread the dough on a baking sheet covered with parchment paper, and garnish it with tomato sauce and fold the edges a little. Add the cheese, then the capers, anchovies and olives.
To finish Bake for about 15 to 20 minutes so that the pizza browns well.

pizza muffins

Ingredients for 10 people
180g flour
3 eggs
8 cl of olive oil
10 cl of milk
1 packet of baking powder
2 slices of white ham
70 g grated Gruyère cheese
2 tomatoes
12 black olives
salt and pepper
oregano
PREPARATION
In a bowl, beat the eggs with the oil and milk. Add flour and baking powder. Mix well. Add the white ham cut into pieces and the grated Gruyère. Salt and pepper. Pour the mixture ? into muffin tins. Arrange tomato pieces, sprinkle with oregano and place a black olive in the center. Bake for 20 minutes at 180°C. Taste!
